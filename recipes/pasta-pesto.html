Pasta with pesto

Description

The following recipe is for a delicious and simple pasta dish. Easy and fast to make, but nutritious and food for the soul. Make this dish with any pasta shape, I prefer it with penne pasta, but use your favorite kind of pasta to make it your own. It works great with all of them!

ingredients

  • 100 gram Penne pasta per person
  • Olive oil or any preferred oil for cooking
  • Add any vegetable you like, I add the green part of leek, you could add a white onion
  • 3 scoops of pesto al verde per person
  • 1 hand full of cherry tomato
  • (Optional)Fresh quartered cherry tomato and parmesan cheese to garnish

Steps

Step 1
Boil a pan of water for the pasta, add a dash of salt
Step 2
Dice your vegetable and heat up oil in a frying pan big enough to fit you pasta later
Step 3
Put your pasta in the boiling water and cook according to the instructions on the packet
Step 4
Fry your leek or onion in the frying pan until softened
Step 5
Dice up your cherry tomato and add to the frying pan
Step 6
Add 3 scoops of pesto per serving to the frying pan
Step 7
Add your boiled pasta to the frying pan and mix all of it thoroughly together
Step 8
Serve on a plate of fitting bowl, you could add some fresh diced cherry tomato and cheese as garnish. Enjoy!
