Kimchi mac and cheese

Description

Spicy twist on a classic "no bake" mac and cheese with some personal tweaks because I am a lazy cook. I make the kimchi myself but any kimchi will work. Furthermore do I use macaroni shells for this pasta, but fussili or penne would also work perfectly.

ingredients

  • 100 grams of pasta per person
  • few tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 1 bacon patch (skip this ingredient for vegetarian dish)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Green part of leek, you could add a white onion (I unfortunately have an allergy to the alloy family so do not add onion or garlic, but feel free to add these in)
  • 250 grams of Kimchi chopped in bite size pieces
  • 1 tbsp gochujang
  • half a tub philadelphia cream cheese
  • shredded gouda cheese
  • shredded parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • (Optional) fresh parsley and cheese for garnish

Steps

Step 1
Boil water in a pan and add the pasta till it is boiled. Drain the pasta and set aside.
Step 2
Put the butter in a frying pan large enough to fit the pasta later and let the butter melt
Step 3
Slice your bacon patch in smaller cubes and fry until browned and crunchy
Step 4
Add a tablespoon of soy sauce for flavour and let it caramelize
Step 5
Add your vegetables and kimchi in the pan and stir to combine
Step 6
Add the gochujang and stir thoroughly. After combined put in the philadelphia and let this melt into the dish, keep stirring.
Step 7
Add the shredded cheeses to the mixture and again stir it to combine.
Step 8
Serve on a plate or fitting bowl, you could add some cheese and fresh parsley for garnish. Enjoy!
